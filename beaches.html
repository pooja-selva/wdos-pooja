Hikkaduwa

Beaches in Hikkaduwa were first discoverd. These have been attracting tourists since the 1960s. The beach further expands and offers many fun activities to keep tourists engaged. These water sports include snorkelling and scuba for adventure seekers. Clear water invites nature lovers who want to swim in the water, witnessing the vibrant, colourful aquatic life. The coral sanctuary offers beautiful coral structures. It hasa wide variety of fishes and large turtles. Coastal islands nurture pockets of wildlife. It sets up a great way to interact with it.

Mirissa

Mirissa is one of the most popular beaches in the south. And for a good reason the area has great waves for surfing, water full of sea creatures, everything from turtles to big Whales. Enjoy fresh coconuts in your hammock and eat delicious food from beach restaurants and loom around on a motorcycle. Four days in the perfect time indulge in the backward culture of this sunny beach town enjoying all the important things to do in Mirissa.

Arugam Bay

Arugam Bay is stunning, with sparkling and beautiful beach with bright white sand and deep blue water. The beach is recognised as a worldwide surf destination because of its attractiveness, which includes some of Sri Lanka's top surf breaks. This reputation not only attracts a large number of people, but it also indicates that the area has a well-developed tourist infrastructure and is easily accessible. The curving beach is reminiscent of a Hawaiian beach, with a dry, sandy section near the ocean, followed by rougher areas with plants and a tree line.
