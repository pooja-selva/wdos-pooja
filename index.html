SRILANKA

Pearl of Indian ocean

Srilanka is an Island country in the South Asia. It is surrounded by the Indian Ocean. Srilanka is famous for it's cinnamon. Colombo, which emerged as the main urban centre during British Rule, remains the executive and judicial capital of Srilanka. The country has divided into 9 Provinces and subdivided into 25 districts.
